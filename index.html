Our Projects

All the objectives of trust have been categorized into three different projects
under which various tasks that are assigned will be taken up and completed under his guidance and protection.

Sai Jyoti

Light of true knowledge

Jyoti is a Sanskrit word that means “light”, Which symbolises knowledge. Knowledge can be gained from education. Sai Jyothi is a project that focuses on education.

Read More

Sai Priti

Joy of serving all living beings

Priti is a Sanskrit word that means "Pleasure or Joy". Sai Prithi is a project, which aims at doing such types of activities that give extreme happiness to Sadguru Sai within us.

Read More

Sai Smriti

Remembrance of SAI by our acts

Smriti is a Sanskrit word which literally means "that which is remembered". The only remembrance of Sai brings many rewards to our lives. Sai Smriti is a project focuses on this.

Read More
